Campaign Journal

The Beginning of the Journey

Our adventure began in the small village of Oakvale, where rumors of ancient ruins had drawn adventurers from across the land. The local tavern, The Prancing Pony, served as our meeting place.

Thorne Ironheart, a sturdy dwarf fighter, was the first to arrive, followed shortly by Lyra Moonshadow, an elven wizard with a keen interest in forgotten magics.

The tavern keeper, an old man named Barley, shared tales of strange lights seen near the eastern woods, where the ruins were said to be located.
